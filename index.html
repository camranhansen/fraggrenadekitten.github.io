Understand Last-Mile deployment pitfalls

After attending the Global Ideas Institute at UofT for the past two years, one overarching theme has been made clear to me: All ideas live or die by the Last Mile. For an idea to gain traction in an area, it has to be more than good. It has to be designed to work in that area, taking into account the difference in culture between where the idea was engineered and where it will be deployed. Even if someone has the greatest idea, it could fail due to problems that arise during the Last Mile.
